Pig Game Rules 🎲

  • The Player 1 starts the game and rolls the dice.
  • If the number showed is different from one, then it is added to his current score and the player can roll the dice again or hold the current score, adding it to his overall score.
  • If the player rolls the dice and the number showed is one, then the player loses his current score and his turn.
  • The winner is the first player to get 100 points. 🥇
